Welcome to the Havoc-OS Wiki!

You can find various information, guides, build instructions and much more here in our wiki.

For users

Get Havoc-OS

  • You can check if your device is on our list of devices.
  • You can look at our information on requesting a device.

Report bugs

  • To grab information needed for a bug report, you can setup ADB and then capture a log.
  • Then, learn where to report your bug by reading our how-to on reporting bugs.

For everyone

Contribute to the wiki

This wiki is powered by Jekyll.

  • You can look at the source on GitHub.
  • And of course, we have a comprehensive guide on contributing.

Build for your device

  • Every officially-supported device has a comprehensive set of build instructions. Simply choose your device from our list of supported devices to get started.

Help

Check our FAQ and useful guides.

For maintainers

Contribute

  • To contribute, you’ll need to be able to produce builds for your device. Pick your device from our list of supported devices to get started.
  • Once you’re successfully running your own build, you can begin to make your changes. All the apps included in Havoc-OS can be found in packages/apps, and core parts of the system can be found in frameworks/base.
  • Once you’ve finished making your change, simply follow our guide on submitting to Gerrit.

Official devices

  • If you unofficially maintain a device, and think it’s good enough to go live, follow our instructions here.
